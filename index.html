About us

About us

PillarBiome applies AI-driven microbiome analysis to understand the intricate interactions between immunity, cancer cells, and gut microbes, providing a comprehensive, science-based assessment of human health. Using this data, we create targeted interventions through food science, active compounds, and precision nutrient blends to optimize cellular function and disease prevention. Our mission is to turn advanced biological insights into measurable, real-world health outcomes.
